About the Project

FREAK is a co-op puzzle adventure game developed through GameDev.ist's competitive 3-month development program. At the end of the program, games were submitted to DeepJam — and FREAK was selected among the top 12 titles.

The game was designed for cooperative play where players must work together to solve environmental puzzles and navigate through an adventure setting. I owned the majority of the technical implementation including networking, character systems, and performance optimization.

Deep Dive
Technical Challenges & Solutions
01Co-op Puzzle State Synchronization
Challenge

Puzzle objects needed to reflect changes from either player instantly and consistently — a desync would break the puzzle logic entirely.

Solution

Used Photon Fusion's server-authoritative NetworkObjects for all puzzle state, with client-side optimistic updates rolled back on conflict to maintain correctness.

023-Month Deadline Pressure
Challenge

Delivering a polished, submission-ready game with networking, puzzles, art, and optimization within a hard 3-month deadline.

Solution

Prioritized core loop first, built modular systems for quick iteration, and cut scope strategically to ensure a complete and polished experience by deadline.
