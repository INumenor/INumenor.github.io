About the Course

At Altın Kariyer Akademi, I designed and delivered a comprehensive game development and Metaverse curriculum targeting students and professionals looking to enter the industry.

The course covered the full spectrum from foundational Unity skills and C# programming to advanced topics like VR/XR development, Blender for game art, Unreal Engine basics, and Metaverse architecture concepts.

Curriculum
What the Course Covers
🎮
Unity & C#

Core Unity engine, scripting fundamentals, component architecture, physics, animation, and building complete game projects from scratch.

🥽
VR / AR / XR

Hands-on VR development covering Oculus/Meta SDK, SteamVR, AR foundations, and cross-platform XR deployment strategies.

🏔️
Unreal Engine

Introduction to Unreal Engine — Blueprints, level design, materials, lighting, and transitioning knowledge between engines.

🧊
Blender for Games

3D modeling, UV unwrapping, texturing, rigging, and exporting game-ready assets from Blender into Unity/Unreal pipelines.

🌐
Metaverse Concepts

Architecture of virtual worlds, social VR design, avatar systems, digital economy, and building interconnected virtual environments.

🎙️
Live Sessions

Monthly interactive live workshops — Q&A, project reviews, debugging sessions, and real-time feedback on student work.
