About the Project

Egg VR is a multiplayer VR experience developed at IronHead Games, a Turkey/USA-based studio. I joined as a full-time developer and took ownership of multiple critical game systems over a 15-month period.

My work spanned from low-level networking with Photon Fusion to high-level game economy design, ensuring a seamless and engaging experience for players across regions.

Technical Challenges & Solutions
01Cross-Region Multiplayer Performance
Challenge

Players from Turkey and the US experiencing high latency and desync in shared VR sessions, causing gameplay breaks.

Solution

Implemented client-side prediction and lag compensation in Photon Fusion, alongside server-authoritative validation to balance responsiveness with fairness.

02IAP Receipt Validation
Challenge

Preventing fraudulent purchases while maintaining a seamless buying experience for legitimate players.

Solution

Integrated server-side receipt validation with retry logic and graceful degradation, achieving near-zero failed legitimate transactions.
